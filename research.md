My research explores the intersection of particle physics, machine learning, and quantum sensing, aiming to unravel fundamental physical laws through advanced computational methodologies.

## **Particle Physics**
Drawing on my experience within the <a href="https://atlas.cern/" target="_blank">ATLAS Collaboration</a> at <a href="https://home.cern/" target="_blank">CERN</a>, my work involves rigorous searches for new physics phenomena and the high-precision measurement of Standard Model processes utilizing data from the Large Hadron Collider (LHC).

- Beyond the Standard Model (BSM): Investigating novel particles and anomalous interactions that challenge the boundaries of current theoretical frameworks.

- Top Quark Physics: Utilizing the heaviest known elementary particle as a stringent probe of fundamental interactions and decay mechanisms.

## **AI4S**
Develop and implement state-of-the-art machine learning architectures to address complex challenges in high-energy physics data analysis.

- Generative Models: Leveraging variational autoencoders (VAEs) and diffusion models to accelerate complex physical simulations.
- Unsupervised Learning: Designing algorithms to discover hidden patterns and rare anomalies within high-dimensional detector data.

## **Statistical Methods**
Develop sophisticated tools for limit setting, significance computation, and rigorous hypothesis testing.
